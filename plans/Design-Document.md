Three Realms Survival

Three Realms Survival is a top-down survival action game where the player must survive
against continuous attacking enemies while collecting experience and upgrading abilities.

The player starts in the Grass Region and can move in eight directions across the map while
enemies spawn continuously and move toward the player. The player automatically attacks
enemies using projectiles, and the goal is to survive as long as possible while defeating
enemies and collecting experience crystals.

When enemies are defeated, they may drop experience crystals or health orbs. The player
must move over these items to collect them. Collecting experience crystals increases the
players experience bar, and when the experience bar is full, the player levels up.

Each time the player levels up, a shop popup appears where the player can choose an
upgrade such as increased damage, movement speed, fire rate, or maximum health. These
upgrades make it easier to survive against stronger enemies that spawn as the game
progresses.

Every 5 levels, a boss appears with special attack patterns. When the boss is defeated, the
player progresses to the next region (Desert Region, then Toxic Region), where enemies
become stronger and faster.

The game ends when:

?  The players health reaches zero (Game Over), or
?  The player defeats the final boss in the final region (Win Condition).

The game includes a scoring system where players earn points for defeating enemies, and
high scores are based on total points remaining after defeating the final boss.
